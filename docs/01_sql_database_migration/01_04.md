# Task 04 - Configure HA/DR

## Introduction

With Tailspin's database workloads now running in Azure SQL Managed Instance and modernized, the next priority is to ensure long‑term resilience. You will also configure high availability (HA) and disaster recovery (DR) to meet Tailspin's strict business continuity requirements. By completing this task, you will strengthen both performance and resilience of the migrated workloads.

## Description

In this task, you will:

1. Configure high availability (HA) to protect against local failures and maintain uptime.
2. Implement disaster recovery (DR) by enabling geo‑replication or failover groups, ensuring resilience against regional outages.
3. Validate HA/DR configurations by simulating failover scenarios and confirming application continuity.

## Success Criteria

- High availability is configured and validated through successful local failover testing.
- Disaster recovery is implemented with geo‑replication or failover groups, and failover to a secondary region is validated.
- Business continuity objectives are met, with minimal downtime during HA/DR validation.
